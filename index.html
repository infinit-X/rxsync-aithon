Target SBU: Hemas Pharmaceuticals & Hospitals. Eliminating patient friction by instantly translating handwritten prescriptions and geo-locating available stock, while proactively managing wholesale pharmaceutical inventory.

1. Value & Impact (30%)

Patients regularly struggle to decipher handwritten prescriptions and frequently visit multiple pharmacies only to find their critical medications are out of stock. RxSync solves this dual friction point through a synchronized AI ecosystem.

⚠ Problem Mastery (20%)

  • ▪
    The Consumer Friction: Illegible prescriptions lead to confusion. Pharmacy-hopping for out-of-stock items delays critical care and degrades the patient experience.
  • ▪
    The Supply Chain Friction: Pharmacy inventory is historically reactive. Sudden regional spikes in specific diseases lead to localized stockouts and expired medicines in other regions.
  • ▪
    The Opportunity: A unified platform where consumer prescription uploads directly inform predictive regional wholesale distribution for Hemas Pharmaceuticals.

⚡ ROI & Strategic Value (10%)

By bridging the gap between patient demand and wholesale supply, Hemas can achieve:

  • Reduced Patient Search Time ~80% Faster
  • Reduction in Expired Stock ~25% Drop
  • Increase in Fulfillment Rate +40% Boost

*Future Scope: Integration with Hemas Hospitals EHR to map patient treatment history to long-term inventory forecasting.

2. The Brain: Innovation & Originality (30%)

The "Primal Edge" of RxSync is its dual-engine approach. It does not just read prescriptions; it acts as a dynamic intelligence layer that learns from out-of-stock events to reshape future regional supply chains.

👁️

Engine A: OCR & NLP

Translates messy, handwritten doctor prescriptions into structured medical data using Optical Character Recognition paired with a medical-specific Large Language Model to identify exact drug names and dosages.

📍

Engine B: Geo-Routing

Once the drug is identified, the AI queries the central database. If available nearby, it routes the user. If unavailable, it dynamically searches a wider radius and assesses "fast delivery" capabilities.

📈

Engine C: Predictive Trend Analysis

Every time a patient searches for an "Out of Stock" drug, the system logs it. The backend AI analyzes these historical trends to predict regional demand spikes, notifying wholesale to redistribute stock proactively.

5. ROI & Strategic Roadmap

By turning consumer "search" data into predictive wholesale supply intelligence, Hemas Pharmaceuticals transitions from a reactive distribution model to a proactive, patient-centric ecosystem.

The Scaling Roadmap

Initially, the database tracks wholesale inventory. Moving forward, pharmacy operators will be given simple portal access to manually flag retail-level stockouts. Ultimately, we plan to integrate this app with Hemas Hospitals EHR.

Phase 1: MVP Integration

Wholesale DB Integration & Customer App OCR verification for high-fidelity routing.

Phase 2: Retail Portal

Retail Pharmacy manual override and notification integration to enrich the predictive dataset.

Phase 3: EHR Sync

Hemas Hospital EHR Sync for longitudinal patient care and chronic medication forecasting.

Data Governance

All prescription images are processed securely and deleted post-extraction to protect PII. Search trends aggregated for predictive analytics are entirely anonymized, ensuring compliance with local data privacy laws.
